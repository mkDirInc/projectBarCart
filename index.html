    Gin Fizz

    Ingredients

    • 2 oz Gin
    • Juice of 1/2 Lemon
    • 1 tsp of powdered sugar
    • Carbonated water

    Instructions

    Shake all ingredients with ice cubes, except soda water. Pour into glass. Top with soda water.
